Designing a Challenge

The fun of Nine Iron Realms comes from its wide array of obstacles and challenges. I designed wind columns, canons, rail-grinds, spinners, and many more.

Developing for Non-Devs

Most of my team does not know how to program, so it’s my job to make sure they don’t have to. I made every obstacle with a custom tool so the level designs and artists can do their jobs without having to touch a single line of code.
